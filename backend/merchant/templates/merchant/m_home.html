Why join Grubmate?

At GrubMate , we believe that great food brings people together. Founded in the heart of Nepal, our mission is to connect food lovers with their favorite local restaurants, cafes, and eateries—delivering delicious meals right to your doorstep, wherever you are. We understand the unique flavors and culinary diversity that Nepal has to offer, from the bustling streets of Kathmandu to the serene towns of Pokhara and beyond.

Our platform is designed to celebrate this diversity, making it easy for you to explore and enjoy a wide range of cuisines, whether you’re craving momos, dal bhat, Newari specialties, or international dishes.
